Welcome to the index page for the Atlassian Connect Static tutorial. To install this add-on in your production instance:

  1. Copy the following link: Add-on descriptor
  2. Navigate to your Atlassian Cloud JIRA instance and navigate to the 'Manage add-ons' administration page
  3. Click on 'Settings' in the bottom left of the Manage add-ons section and "Enable development mode"
  4. Click on 'Upload add-on' and paste in the link to the Add-on descriptor that you copied from the first step.

Congratulations, you have now installed this Atlassian Connect add-on on your Atlassian Cloud production instance.
